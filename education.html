"Water Governance and Climate Adaptation in Bengaluru: Examining Policy Barriers and Institutional Responses"

My doctoral research investigates the complex dynamics of water governance in rapidly urbanizing cities, with Bengaluru, India as a case study. This interdisciplinary work combines policy analysis, stakeholder engagement, and geospatial methods to understand how institutional frameworks respond to climate-induced water challenges.
